MHB Brakes - Small Parts, Big Stopping Power

At MHB Brakes, we specialize in fixing and replacing small and medium-sized vehicle brake parts, including brake boosters, calipers, brake pads, brake drums, brake valves, and master cylinders. We know that a vehicle's brakes are one of the most critical components for safety on the road, which is why we take great pride in providing reliable brake repairs and replacements. Our team of experienced technicians are experts in all types of brake systems, from the latest models to classic cars. We use only the highest quality parts and state-of-the-art equipment to ensure that every job is done right, the first time. Our attention to detail and commitment to quality means you can trust MHB Brakes for all your brake repair needs.
We understand that vehicle repairs can be stressful, which is why we make every effort to provide a hassle-free and convenient experience for our customers. We offer flexible appointment scheduling, fair and transparent pricing, and a friendly and knowledgeable staff to answer any questions you may have.

Regular brake system inspections are important to catch potential problems before they lead to brake failure. MHB Brakes can perform thorough inspections to ensure that brake systems are functioning correctly.
